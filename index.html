HOW-IT-WORKS

HOW-IT-WORKS


JellyTeam will provide the initial marketing funds & handle the entire back-end of the mint so the project team can focus on building.
Revenue of every project launched under JellyPad will be allocated by the JellyTeam in the illustrated breakdown below.
JellyTeam withholds a final 20% of mint revenue until the team has delivered on their first major roadmap.
This 20% would serve as insurance/compensation in the unlikely case of a rug-pull.

JellyFund (10-15%)

JellyTeam (10-15%)

Project Funds (50%) - Marketing & Team Etc

Project Funds (20%) - Insurance For Minters

About JellyAcademy

JellyAcademy is an initiative started by the team behind JellyBabies to help all that are new to the Solana Blockchain to get started. Through JellyAcademy, community members can tap on JellyPad to launch your own NFT collection.
